Our story

We're making beauty bookings effortless.

STYLO connects customers with trusted beauty and grooming professionals — fairly, simply, and locally.

Talk to the team

Built for the real world.

STYLO started in 2024 when we saw too many beauty and grooming businesses lose clients to missed calls, no-shows, and clumsy booking systems. We built a fair marketplace where customers book in seconds and pros keep 100%.

From mobile-first discovery to home-service appointments, we make it simpler to stay booked up and to feel confident about every appointment.
